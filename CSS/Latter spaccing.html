Maldives

Comprising a territory spanning roughly 90,000 square kilometres (35,000 sq mi) including the sea, and a land area of 298 square kilometres (115 sq mi), the Maldives is the smallest country in Asia, as well as one of the world's most geographically dispersed sovereign states, and the smallest Muslim-majority countries by land area. With a population of 515,132 in the 2022 census, it is the 2nd least populous country in Asia.
